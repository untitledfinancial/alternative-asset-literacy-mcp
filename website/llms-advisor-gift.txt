## How to Use the MCP Tool

The `advisor.gift_bundle` tool on the Alternative Asset Literacy MCP server generates a complete client gift bundle on demand.

**MCP Server:** https://alternativeassetliteracy.com/mcp
**Tool:** `advisor.gift_bundle`

**Input:**
- `client_description` (required): Free-text description of the client — their background, meeting context, knowledge gaps
- `topic` (optional): Focus override — e.g. "DeFi", "ESG", "art investing", "alternative assets"

**Example inputs:**
- "HNW woman who just sold her biotech company, meeting with UBS next month"
- "DeFi-curious client asking about yield farming and staking"
- "ESG-focused client worried about greenwashing in her fund"
- "Art collector client considering an art fund"
- "New client inheriting family wealth, no prior investment experience"

**Output includes:**
- Client profile matched (e.g. HNW Women Investor, DeFi Investor, ESG Investor)
- Free entry point — Investing Primer, no account required
- 3-module learning path with "why this matters for your client" for each module
- 3 key terms the client should know before their next meeting (with definitions)
- Toolkit highlights — relevant tools for that client type
- Suggested advisor message — copy-paste text the advisor can send directly to the client
- Pricing and app download link

## Pre-Built Gift Bundles by Client Type

### HNW Women Investor (Post-Liquidity Event, Private Banking Client)

**Learning Path:**
1. **Investing Primer** (Free, 45 min) — Foundation for every conversation about complex portfolios. Covers LP/GP structures, carried interest, and the vocabulary advisory meetings assume clients already know.
2. **Alternative Investing** (45 min) — Private equity, venture capital, hedge funds, real estate. The J-curve, capital calls, fee waterfalls. Arrives three steps ahead.
3. **Behavioral Economics** (45 min) — The psychological layer advisors cannot fix alone. Loss aversion, anchoring, overconfidence — she'll recognize her own patterns before they create problems.

**Key Terms:** Carried interest, J-curve, capital call, loss aversion, family office

**Suggested Message:**
"I wanted to send you something ahead of our next meeting — a resource I recommend to clients who want to arrive at these conversations as a peer, not a recipient. Alternative Asset Literacy covers the asset classes we'll be discussing. The Investing Primer is free — no account needed. The more prepared you are, the better our time together will be."

### DeFi-Curious Client

**Learning Path:**
1. **Investing Primer** (Free, 45 min) — Establishes foundational vocabulary before DeFi-specific concepts.
2. **Decentralized Finance** (45 min) — Blockchain, smart contracts, stablecoins, DAOs, CBDCs. The difference between speculation and protocol-level understanding.
3. **DeFi Investing** (90 min) — Yield farming, staking, liquidity provision, and 14 advisor Q&As built specifically for the wealth management conversation around digital assets.

**Key Terms:** Smart contract, stablecoin, impermanent loss, yield farming, CBDC

---

### ESG-Focused Client

**Learning Path:**
1. **Investing Primer** (Free, 45 min) — Foundation before ESG specifics.
2. **Climate, Energy & Real World Assets** (45 min) — Green bonds, carbon credits, EU Taxonomy, SFDR Article 8 vs 9 — the tools to evaluate whether an ESG fund's methodology matches its marketing.
3. **Behavioral Economics** (45 min) — Confirmation bias and greenwashing recognition. Clients who understand their own pattern-seeking arrive more skeptical of ESG marketing claims.

**Key Terms:** Greenwashing, SFDR, green bonds, carbon credits, TCFD

---

### Art Collector Client

**Learning Path:**
1. **Investing Primer** (Free, 45 min) — Alternative asset vocabulary before art-specific mechanics.
2. **Art as Investment** (45 min) — Auction mechanics, hammer price, buyer's premium, provenance, fractional ownership, art-secured lending. The financial layer of a serious collection.
3. **Behavioral Economics** (45 min) — Art buying is extraordinarily emotional. Anchoring, loss aversion, and social proof drive auction decisions.

**Key Terms:** Hammer price, buyer's premium, provenance

## Toolkit — What Clients Get at the Premium Tier

**Advisor Question Sets** — Scored evaluation tools teaching clients what a competent advisory answer looks like for DeFi, ESG, and alternative assets. Not adversarial — educational. Clients arrive with better questions.
