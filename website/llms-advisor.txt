## What This Platform Provides

### Advisor Questions (Toolkit — Premium)

Interactive "Questions for Your Financial Advisor" — scored evaluation tools with checkboxes, competency ratings, "Why This Matters" context reveals for each question, and personal notes fields.

**Three scored evaluations:**

**DeFi Advisor Fluency** — Does your advisor understand blockchain, smart contracts, stablecoin risk, regulatory status, and the difference between speculation and protocol-level investing? Questions include: How would you explain impermanent loss? What is your framework for evaluating DeFi protocol risk? How do you stay current with crypto regulation?

**ESG Advisor Competency** — Can your advisor distinguish genuine impact from greenwashing? Do they understand the EU Taxonomy, TCFD, and SFDR? Can they explain the difference between Article 8 and Article 9 funds? Questions include: How do you evaluate an ESG fund's methodology? What is your view on carbon credits as an offset strategy? How do you screen for greenwashing in fund selection?

**Alternative Assets Fluency** — Does your advisor understand LP/GP structures, fee waterfalls, the J-curve, capital calls, and liquidity risk? Questions include: How do you think about alternative asset allocation as a percentage of total portfolio? What is your due diligence process for evaluating a private equity fund manager? How do you explain carried interest to clients?

Each question is rated on a scale from Inadequate to Competent response, with notes on what the difference looks like.

### Universal Due Diligence Checklist

Essential questions to ask before committing capital to any alternative investment:
- Structure: How is this fund structured? What are my rights as an LP?
- Fees: What is the full fee waterfall — management fee, carry, expenses, advisor commission?
- Liquidity: What is the exit mechanism? Under what conditions is it restricted?
- Manager: What is the track record? How was it audited? What was the worst vintage year?
- Alignment: How is the GP's own capital invested in this fund?
- Legal: What are the key risk factors in the PPM? What triggers a clawback?
- Reporting: How often will I receive statements? In what format?
- Taxes: What are the tax implications of this structure for my situation?

### Red Flag Framework

Warning signs across alternative asset classes:
- Pressure to decide before you have read the documents
- Returns that seem uncorrelated with any known risk factor
- Advisor who cannot explain the fee structure in plain language
- No audited track record, or track record that cherry-picks vintage years
- Lock-up period with no clear rationale
- Manager who discourages questions about liquidity
- ESG fund with no methodology documentation
- DeFi protocol with unaudited smart contracts
- Art fund with no independent valuation process

### Advisory Team Guide

Who you need and what they do:
- **Investment Advisor (RIA)** — fiduciary, manages the investment strategy
- **Estate Attorney** — wills, trusts, beneficiary designations, generational transfer
- **Tax Advisor (CPA/CFP)** — alternative asset tax implications, carried interest, K-1s
- **Family Office Consultant** — governance structures, investment policy statements
- **Art Advisor** — collection strategy, auction representation, provenance research
- **Insurance Advisor** — art insurance, liability, umbrella coverage for significant assets
