Privacy Policy

Effective March 24, 2026

This page covers our Privacy Policy. For our Terms of Use, including disclaimers and regulatory disclosures, please see our Terms of Use. By using our application or website, you agree to both policies.

1. Who We Are

Alternative Asset Literacy is an application and website operated by Untitled_ LuxPerpetua Technologies, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"). We are the data controller for information processed through our app and website.

Contact: Contact form

2. Information We Collect

2a. iOS Application (On-Device Only)

The iOS app stores the following data locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). This data is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Display name (optional, first name only) — used to personalize greetings
  • Learning progress — completed modules, sections, quiz scores, and time spent
  • Reflection journal entries — free-text entries you write about your learning
  • Display preferences — font style, text size, emoji preferences, course title

All app data remains on your device. We have no access to it and cannot retrieve it.

2b. Website

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Email address — if you voluntarily submit it through our signup form
  • Interest preferences — topic selections you make on the signup form
  • Standard server logs — IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps, collected automatically by our hosting provider (Cloudflare)

3. How We Use Information

  • App data: Used solely to personalize your learning experience and track progress. Never transmitted off-device.
  • Email addresses: Used to send you updates about the app, educational content, and launch notifications. We do not sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Server logs: Used for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and aggregated analytics (no individual tracking).

4. Third-Party Services

This application connects to third-party services solely for content delivery and payment processing. No personal user data is transmitted to these services. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are integrated into this application or website. No cookies are set for tracking purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • iOS app data: Stored locally on your device, protected by iOS Data Protection. You can delete all app data at any time via Settings → "Reset All Progress."
  • Website signup data: Stored in Airtable, which maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance and encrypts data at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Data retention: Email signups are retained until you request deletion. App data is retained on your device until you delete it.

6. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to any third party. We do not share your data with data brokers. We do not use your data for targeted advertising. This applies to all users regardless of location.

7. Your Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You may request a copy of the personal information we have collected about you in the preceding 12 months.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete any personal information we have collected from you.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information. No opt-out is necessary, but we honor all such requests.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, use our contact form. Select "Privacy / Data Request" as the subject. We will respond within 45 days.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Economic Area Residents)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides you with the following rights:

  • Lawful Basis: We process your email address based on your consent (provided when you submit the signup form). App data is processed based on legitimate interest (providing the service you requested).
  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You may object to processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent at any time via our contact form.

To exercise these rights, use our contact form and select "Privacy / Data Request." We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

9. Children's Privacy

This application and website are not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will promptly delete it.
