## Issues

### Subtitle is over the 30-character limit
"Invest With Clarity & Confidence" is 32 characters. Apple enforces 30 max. If this is live it may have been truncated or rejected silently. Recommended fix below.

### Keywords are underoptimized
Current keywords waste characters on generic, high-competition terms ("financial literacy", "wealth", "portfolio") that the app will never rank for against established players. No terms targeting the specific, lower-competition intents where AAL can actually win: private equity education, family office, art investing, accredited investor.

### Investing Primer free tier not called out
The free tier is a significant conversion lever — users discover a free offering and convert. Currently not mentioned in the subtitle or keywords.
