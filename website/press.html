App Description_

One-liner:
Alternative Asset Literacy is an iOS app that teaches modern investors — accredited and non-accredited — how to navigate alternative investments, DeFi, ESG, art markets, and behavioral finance.

Short description (100 words):

Alternative Asset Literacy is a financial education iOS app built for investors who want to understand the full landscape of modern asset classes — not just stocks and bonds. The app covers private equity, decentralized finance (DeFi), ESG and climate investing, art and collectibles, behavioral economics, and gender lens investing across nine structured learning modules. The Investing Primer is free with no account required. Built by Untitled_ LuxPerpetua Technologies, Inc., the app draws from a 186-source research library and a 351-term glossary. Available on the App Store.

Extended description (200 words):

Alternative Asset Literacy is a financial education iOS app designed for the investor who has been left out of the conversation — women entering wealth, new entrepreneurs, and anyone navigating financial infrastructure that was not built with them in mind.

The app covers nine modules: an Investing Primer (free), Alternative Assets, Behavioral Economics, Gender Lens Investing, Decentralized Finance, Art Investing, ESG & Climate, DeFi Investing, and the Kahlo & Basquiat module on cultural capital and the art market. Each module is research-backed, drawing from a 186-source library that includes policy papers from the IFC, SEC, UN PRI, TCFD, and peer-reviewed academic research.

Alternative asset categories covered in the app — including DeFi protocols, public ESG funds, and climate vehicles — are available to all investors regardless of accredited status. The app does not require an account to access the free Investing Primer.

Built on iOS 26 with a Dutch Golden Age design system. Developed by Untitled_ LuxPerpetua Technologies, Inc., a woman-founded fintech education company.

Founder_

Victoria Lee Case is the founder and CEO of Untitled_ LuxPerpetua Technologies, Inc. She built Alternative Asset Literacy to close the financial education gap for investors navigating an asset landscape that was not designed with them in mind — particularly women entering wealth, new entrepreneurs, and modern investors exploring alternatives beyond traditional markets.

About Untitled_ LuxPerpetua Technologies, Inc._

Untitled_ LuxPerpetua Technologies, Inc. is a woman-founded fintech education company building financial literacy tools for modern investors. Its flagship product, Alternative Asset Literacy, is an iOS app covering private equity, DeFi, ESG, art markets, behavioral economics, and gender lens investing — drawing from a 186-source research library. The company's mission is to close the financial education gap for investors who have historically been underserved by traditional financial infrastructure.
