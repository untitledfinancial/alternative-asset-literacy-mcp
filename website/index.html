About

Alternative Asset Literacy offers accessible, research-backed modules that teach the foundations of modern investing—spanning behavioral economics, gender and financial decision-making, alternative assets, art, crypto, ESG, and more. Designed as a beautiful, easy-to-use iOS app, our curriculum helps you understand how money, psychology, and markets interact so you can make smarter, more confident financial decisions.

This application and all content are provided exclusively for educational and informational purposes. Nothing constitutes financial advice, investment advice, or any form of professional advisory service. Consult with a qualified financial advisor before making any investment decisions.
